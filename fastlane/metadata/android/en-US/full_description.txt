A simple and intuitive scoreboard for the card game Belote. Keep track of scores for two teams, view round history, and enjoy a custom keyboard designed for quick score entry.

Features:
• Easy score entry for each round
• Handles "TK" scores for failed contracts
• Automatically calculates total scores
• Displays a history of all rounds
• Allows editing the last entered round
• Win detection at 301 points with a "New Game" dialog
• Custom keyboard optimized for Belote scoring
• Dark mode support
• Screen stays on during gameplay
• Automatic game state restoration

Perfect for keeping score during your Belote games without pen and paper!
